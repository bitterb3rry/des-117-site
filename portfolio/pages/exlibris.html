Ex Libris

  • Medium: Digital, Illustrator
  • Size: 500px x 500px
  • Class: DES 145 Graphic Design History

Ex Libris means the "library of". This bookplate cover was designed as a moderm twist to a SEND magazine cover where the issue was coving the topic of computer art issues. At the time, making designs with computers was a very new concept. Fast forward to today, technology like tablets have enabled so many people to try out art and design on their own without much experience.

The assignment was to create a bookplate that is personalized yet following a graphic design history artifact. The bookplate aimed to keep a retro color palette while updating some of the titles to better fit today. The date Nov 2019 was when I got my first tablet and $750 was how much it costed me.

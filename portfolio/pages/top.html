3D Top

  • Medium: CAD, Rhino
  • Class: DES 150A CAD for Designers

This top was designed by playing with circles in CAD. The body composes of 2 rings of 6 circles connected and attached to the stem through the diagonal oval towards the bottom. The bottom tip of the top is a sphere which allows the weight to be distributed better than if it was a upside down cone. The final product was able to be printed and it spins for about 10 seconds before slowing down.
